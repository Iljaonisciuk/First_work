Intellectual Property Development Institute

    Intellectual Property Development Institute (IPDI) manages the portfolio of intellectual property and organises the registration, evaluation and successful international sales of patents on inventions.

    The Institute specialises in many areas of inventions, whereas its strongest fields are: hydrodynamics, robotics, environmentally friendly electricity production and thermomechanics. The activities of the Institute in Lithuania have been made highly effective by high class professionals and, moreover, the registration fees of patents on inventions are among the world`s lowest.

    The Institute cooperates with business analysts, economists, lawyers and administrators. International projects are implemented in collaboration with other institutes based in Switzerland, China, Russia, Austria and other countries.
